CAMT: College of Arts Media and Technology

Background

Chiang Mai University, located in the northern part of Thailand, is academically prepared to advance into international studies and research. The university provides academic support and facilities to strengthen the knowledge-based economy and society.
In 2003, the University Council approved the establishment of the College of Technology, Media, and ICT, and in 2006, the name was changed to the "College of Arts, Media, and Technology." The college also supports side projects and is evaluated by government agencies.
